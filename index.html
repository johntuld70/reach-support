About the App

Reach helps you organize meetups with friends without the back-and-forth. Create an event, invite people, and let everyone coordinate in one place.

  • Create and manage events easily
  • Invite friends with a single tap
  • Poll your group on times and locations
  • Track who is coming in real time

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event?
After logging in, tap the + button on the home screen and fill in the event details.

How do I invite friends?
Inside any event, tap Invite and share the link or enter your friends email addresses.

Is Reach free?
Yes, Reach is free to download and use.

Which platforms is Reach available on?
Reach is available on iOS. Android coming soon.
